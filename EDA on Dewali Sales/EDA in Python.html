Conclusion:¶

Based on the exploratory data analysis (EDA) of the dataset, it can be concluded that most buyers are married women aged between 26 and 35 years, residing in Uttar Pradesh, Maharashtra, and Karnataka, and employed in the sectors of Information Technology, Healthcare, and Aviation, demonstrate a stronger inclination towards purchasing products from the Food, Clothing, and Electronics categories.¶
